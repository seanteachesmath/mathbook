Cobalt-60 is a radioactive isotope used in the treatment of cancer. A \(500\)-milligram sample of cobalt-60 decays to \(385\) milligrams after \(2\) years.

  2. Write a decay law \(N(t) = N_0e^{kt}\) for cobalt-60.
