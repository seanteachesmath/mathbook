Section 7 Miscellaneous

A circumflex should still be obtainable with circum: . And ca. is now ca..

LaTeX's ten reserved characters once each had an empty element of its own: # $ % ^ & _ { } ~ \.

And similarly, nine other elements were made redundant: < > [ ] * / ` {braces} [brackets].

Tables once looked like this, but now are really different.
