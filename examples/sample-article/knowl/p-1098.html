If you use the unstructured form of a division, and have both inline and divisional exercises, there is a potential to form ambiguous cross-references. To wit, check that 32.2 and 32.1.2 are really different exercises (which you are unable to do if you are reading this in print!). The solution is to include the type of exercise in the reference, which will assist everybody, but especially your print readers: Checkpoint 32.2 and Exercise 32.1.2.
